Obtaining Geographic Coordinates with Google Map

  1. Go to Google Maps and find your location.
  2. Right-click on the map corresponding to your location. You will see a pop-up menu with the Geographic Coordinates displayed at the top. The coordinates are represented by two decimal numbers separated by a comma.
  3. Click on the coordinates to copy them to the clipboard, then paste them into the "Geographic Coordinates" field of the plugin settings (under "Localization"). Click "Save".

That's it! You have provided information about your location to the Now Playing plugin. Next, you should set the "Timezone" value to "Match Geographic Coordinates". The plugin will then be able to display the time and date, as well as the weather, relevant to your location.
